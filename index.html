Directions:

Controls:

[Right Click] = Interact with buttons

In Game Controls:
[W/A/S/D] = Move around
[M] = Pause the game
[1-7] = Select Tower To Place
Hold [E] = Place tower at cursor
(Right Click to place tower while holding E)
[Right Click] = Select placed tower

While Tower Is Selected:
[U] = Upgrade Tower
[Backspace] = Sell tower
[L/K] = Switch tower targetting

Your goal:

In this game, your goal is to fight off the waves of enemy cubes and shapes using the towers you are given. Each and every tower has its own unique purpose, whether it be a different passive effect, or type of bullet, so make sure to use all towers to maximize damage. The rounds will get increasingly more difficult as you progress, which also will give more money. This money can be used to upgrade your towers, giving them buffs from detection of new types or more damage or even new passives. Each map and difficulty are unique, introducing new enemies or new paths. Make sure to enjoy and try to get as far as you can! (Hint: the flamethrower is a great early game tower)

KEYBOARD AND MOUSE IS REQUIRED TO PLAY
